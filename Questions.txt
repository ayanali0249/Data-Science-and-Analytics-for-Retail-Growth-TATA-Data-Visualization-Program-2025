# Online Retail Data Analysis Questions

## Question 1: Revenue Time Series for 2011 (Monthly)
The CEO of the retail store is interested in viewing the time series of the revenue data for the year 2011 only. The analysis should:
- Focus on revenue for each month of 2011.
- Highlight seasonal trends.
- Include reasons behind the trends to help forecast for the next year.

## Question 2: Top 10 Countries by Revenue (Excluding UK)
The CMO wants to:
- View the top 10 countries generating the highest revenue.
- Include the quantity sold along with the revenue generated.
- Exclude the United Kingdom from this visual.

## Question 3: Top 10 Customers by Revenue
The CMO is interested in:
- Viewing the top 10 customers ranked by revenue.
- Visualizing the greatest revenue-generating customer at the start, gradually declining to lower revenue-generating customers.
- Targeting high-revenue customers to maintain their satisfaction.

## Question 4: Product Demand by Region (Excluding UK)
The CEO wants to:
- Identify regions (all countries except UK) with the greatest demand for their products.
- Visualize the entire data in a single view without scrolling or hovering over data points.
- Use these insights to initiate an expansion strategy in high-demand regions.
